    Gravity Drift: A Space-Themed Rocket Launch Game

    Experience the thrill of launching rockets in Gravity Drift, a captivating space game where you navigate through a dynamic asteroid field around celestial bodies. Plan your trajectory, manage your fuel, and aim for the planets while avoiding collisions with asteroids and the sun.

    How to Play Gravity Drift

    Click the "Start Game" button to launch your rockets from Earth. Observe the gravitational pull of the sun and planets, and strategically adjust your rockets' paths. Your goal is to land as many rockets as possible on the planets while conserving fuel and dodging asteroids. The game ends when all rockets have either reached a planet, drifted out of fuel, or collided. Compete for the best score on the leaderboard based on landing order, fuel efficiency, and distance traveled.

    Features

    • Realistic gravity simulation around the sun and planets
    • Challenging asteroid field to test your navigation skills
    • Strategic fuel management for long-distance trajectories
    • Dynamic leaderboard to track your best performances
    • Customizable rocket names for a personalized gaming experience
