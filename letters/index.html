How to Play & Match

  • Set Your List: Use the settings to enter the names you want to include in the game.
  • Letters Fall: Letters needed for the available names will start dropping from the top of the pinball machine.
  • Type to Match: As letters enter the "Target Zone" at the bottom, they contribute to forming a name. If the sequence of letters matches the start of one or more names in your list, the buffer in the target zone will update.
  • Complete Names: Successfully type out a full name, and it gets marked as "matched," disappearing from the active list.
  • Clear the Board: The game continues until all names have been successfully matched and cleared from the board.
