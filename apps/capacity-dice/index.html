    Why Capacity Planner Dice?

    Teams rarely enjoy discussing “surprise PTO” or “meeting storms.” Capacity Planner Dice makes it easy: roll a few dice, talk about each card, then log the adjusted plan so everyone has the same expectation. It works for scrum, kanban, or any hybrid planning ritual.

    • Faster alignment: Have the tough capacity conversation in under a minute.
    • Shareable: Copy the summary into Jira, Confluence, or Slack.
    • Customizable: Toggle positive boosts or wildcards depending on your sprint focus.
    • Repeatable: Keep the history visible so your team can compare rolls across ceremonies.
