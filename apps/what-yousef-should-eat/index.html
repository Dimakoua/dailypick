1. What is the What Yousef Should Eat wheel?

What Yousef Should Eat is a personalised Iranian food spinner built for anyone who loves Persian cuisine and wants a quick, playful way to decide what to eat. It covers everything from hearty khoresh stews and saffron-perfumed rice dishes to street-side kababs and beloved Iranian sweets.

2. Who should use this wheel?

This wheel is made for fans of Iranian food who find themselves lost between too many delicious options. Whether you are cooking at home, browsing a Persian restaurant menu, or planning a grocery run for the week, the wheel makes the choice for you — fast and fair.

3. How it works

Select one of the four Iranian food category presets, then hit the spin button. The wheel lands on a dish and displays it with a نوش جان! — Persian for "bon appétit". You can spin as many times as you like until you find something that feels right.

4. Features

  • Four Persian presets: Persian Classics, Kabab Night, Rice Dishes, and Iranian Snacks.
  • Authentic dish names: Items are real, well-known Iranian dishes used in everyday cooking.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • نوش جان result display: Winner is shown with Persian enthusiasm after every spin.

5. Iranian food categories

Persian Classics covers iconic khoresh stews and soups — Ghormeh Sabzi, Fesenjan, Ash Reshteh, and Abgoosht. Kabab Night includes Koobideh, Joojeh, Barg, and Shishlik. Rice Dishes features saffron-infused polo combinations like Zereshk Polo, Baghali Polo, and the beloved Tahdig. Iranian Snacks rounds out the experience with Lavashak, Ajil, Sohan, Gaz, and Bastani Sonnati.

6. Tips

Use Persian Classics for a full weeknight dinner, Kabab Night when hosting guests, and Iranian Snacks for an afternoon treat. Mix the custom wheel feature with your local Persian restaurant's menu items for the most practical daily use.
