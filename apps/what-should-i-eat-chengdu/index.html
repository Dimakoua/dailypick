1. What is the Chengdu Food Wheel?

Chengdu Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s famous Sichuan cuisine. It blends spicy hot pot, street food, and local favorites into one playful decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Chengdu locals choosing dinner, visitors exploring the city’s food culture, and anyone who wants a bold meal idea. Spin for a fresh Chengdu food suggestion.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then enjoy. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Spicy Sichuan Classics, Local Favorites, Street & Snack Eats, and Global Choices.
  • Real Chengdu picks: Every item reflects the city’s bold and numbing flavors.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Chengdu-inspired tones.

5. Chengdu food categories

Spicy Sichuan Classics covers Chengdu hot pot, mala noodles, mapo tofu, spicy rabbit, dan dan noodles, and kung pao chicken. Local Favorites includes Sichuan fish, braised pork, cold noodles, pickled vegetables, and tea-smoked duck. Street & Snack Eats highlights Chengdu’s street food scene. Global Choices brings international dining into the city.

6. Tips

Choose Spicy Sichuan Classics for bold flavor, Local Favorites for regional specialties, Street & Snack Eats for quick bites, and Global Choices for variety. Personalize the wheel with your favorite Chengdu spots if you want.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific route, try the Chinese or street-food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Chengdu blends bold spice, numbing Sichuan flavors, and rich street food. This wheel simplifies that abundance into one fast, fun dining decision.
