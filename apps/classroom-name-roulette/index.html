Classroom Name Roulette

A fair, fun way to call on students. Enter your class roster and let the roulette decide who's next!

Called students: None

How to use the Random Student Caller

This tool is designed to make classroom participation more engaging and equitable. By using a no-repeat name picker, you can ensure that every student gets a chance to participate before the list resets.

Key Features for Teachers:

  • Fair Selection: Removes "favoritism" bias by letting the computer choose.
  • No-Repeat Mode: Toggle the "Remove after calling" checkbox to cycle through your entire class roster.
  • Save Your List: Your student names are saved in your browser, so they'll be here the next time you open the app.

Why Randomize Student Calling?

Randomly calling on students (often called "cold calling") keeps the class alert and ensures that quieter students have their voices heard. It creates a "gamified" atmosphere where the anticipation of the draw makes learning more interactive.
