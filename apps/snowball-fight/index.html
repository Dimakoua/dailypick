      How to Play

      1. Create or Join: Start a new room or join an existing one with a shared link.
      2. Set Your Name: Enter your name so teammates know who you are.
      3. Battle! Move with WASD/Arrow keys, throw snowballs with mouse clicks or spacebar.
      4. Survive: You have 5 health points. Get hit 5 times and you're out!
      5. Leaderboard: First to die appears at the top, last survivor at the bottom.

      Snowball Fight – multiplayer battle arena for remote team energizers

      Snowball Fight is a browser-based multiplayer game that brings classic battle arena action to remote teams. It helps groups recharge with a quick, competitive snowball match that requires no downloads.

      Use this game as a virtual icebreaker, a sprint break, or a fun team-building activity where everyone can join from their browser.

      The app supports rooms, shared leaderboards, and real-time gameplay so teams stay engaged and active.

      1. What Is Snowball Fight?

      Snowball Fight is a real-time multiplayer battle game where players dodge snowballs, compete for survival, and aim to be the last person standing. The primary keyword is snowball fight, and the game is built to bring playful energy to remote team sessions.

      Players join a room with their name, share the invite link, and then battle in a fast-paced arena with simple controls.

      This makes it an ideal choice for team building, warmups, and quick virtual events.

      2. How to Use It?

      1. Enter your display name and create or join a room.
      2. Share the invite link with teammates so everyone can join the battle.

      3. Key Features & Customization

      • Real-time multiplayer: play with teammates in a shared room experience.
      • Room invites: share links to get players into the battle quickly.
      • Death order leaderboard: track who lasted the longest in each match.
      • Browser-based play: no downloads or installs needed for instant access.
      • Accessible controls: simple keyboard and mouse inputs make it easy for anyone to jump in.

      4. Multiplayer Arena Action

      Snowball Fight turns virtual team play into a lively arena battle. Players move through the field, dodge incoming snowballs, and score by staying alive longer than others.

      The death order leaderboard creates a clear result so everyone can see the outcome of each round, making it great for competitive sessions and fun team breaks.

      The quick match pace keeps the game short and replayable, perfect for a break between meetings or a fast team challenge.

      5. Sharing and Exporting

      Snowball Fight does not currently export match results directly, but session links make it easy to invite teammates and continue play. Share the battle room with your team and use the leaderboard as the official match result.

      The game is built for instant use, so sharing the page link is the simplest way to get everyone participating.

      6. When to Use This Game?

      Use Snowball Fight when you want a quick, engaging break that gets your team active and laughing. It works especially well for remote groups that need a playful moment to recharge.

      • During standups or sprint breaks to boost energy.
      • As a team icebreaker or warmup activity.
      • When you want a fun, multiplayer challenge for remote meetings.
      • For quick competitive sessions that don't require setup.
      • To bring nostalgia and game-style action into team rituals.
