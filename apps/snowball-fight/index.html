      How to Play

      1. Create or Join: Start a new room or join an existing one with a shared link.
      2. Set Your Name: Enter your name so teammates know who you are.
      3. Battle! Move with WASD/Arrow keys, throw snowballs with mouse clicks or spacebar.
      4. Survive: You have 5 health points. Get hit 5 times and you're out!
      5. Leaderboard: First to die appears at the top, last survivor at the bottom.

      Classic Multiplayer Snowball Fight for Teams

      Snowball Fight brings the nostalgic thrill of classic browser battle games to your remote team. Jump into a fast-paced arena, dodge snowballs, and compete to be the last player standing.

      Easy Team Setup and Shared Leaderboards

      Create or join a room, enter your name, and invite teammates with a link. The real-time death order leaderboard keeps everyone engaged and makes this a perfect activity for quick breaks or virtual icebreakers.

      Play Together Without Downloading Anything

      This browser-based multiplayer game works instantly and supports remote collaboration across devices. No installs required, just load the room and start the battle.
