Need a fair, instant way to pick a winner, assign tasks, or settle a bet?

Use this random number generator to instantly pick an integer between your chosen minimum and maximum. Set your range, hit generate, and get your answer—no sign-ups, no hassle.

How to Use It

  1. Set the minimum value in the Minimum input field.
  2. Set the maximum value in the Maximum input field.
  1. Click Generate to get a random integer within the inclusive range.
  1. Click Reset to restore the default range and clear the result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I generate negative numbers?

Yes, you can enter negative numbers in either the minimum or maximum fields to generate a random negative integer.

Is the result truly random?

The generator uses your browser's built-in math function to pick a pseudo-random integer. It is perfectly fair for giveaways, games, and quick decisions.

What happens if I set the minimum higher than the maximum?

The tool will automatically detect the higher and lower values and correct the range for you behind the scenes, ensuring you always get a valid result.
