1. What is the Shanghai Food Wheel?

Shanghai Food Wheel is a meal picker built for the city’s cosmopolitan and traditional food culture. It combines Shanghai classics, Chinese favorites, and international eats into one playful decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Shanghai locals choosing a meal, visitors exploring the city’s food scene, and anyone looking to simplify dining choices. Spin for a fresh Shanghai food idea.

3. How it works

Select a preset then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then enjoy. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Shanghai Classics, Chinese Favorites, Street & Global Eats, and City Dining.
  • Real Shanghai picks: Every item reflects the city’s historic cuisine and modern restaurants.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Shanghai-inspired tones.

5. Shanghai food categories

Shanghai Classics includes xiaolongbao, shengjian, hairy crab, drunken chicken, red-braised pork, and sweet soup dumplings. Chinese Favorites covers beloved flavors like mapo tofu, kung pao, hot pot, and tea-smoked duck. Street & Global Eats highlights market snacks, fusion dishes, and popular international meals. City Dining points to Shanghai’s upscale restaurants, cafés, and riverfront dining.

6. Tips

Choose Shanghai Classics for local specialties, Chinese Favorites for well-known Chinese dishes, Street & Global Eats for a mix of market food and international options, and City Dining for restaurant-style meals. Add your favorite Shanghai restaurants to personalize the wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific route, try the Chinese or street-food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Shanghai blends historic culinary tradition with modern global dining. This wheel simplifies the city’s dining abundance into one fast, fun decision.
