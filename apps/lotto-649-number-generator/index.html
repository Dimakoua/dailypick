    1. What Is the Lotto 6/49 Number Generator?

    Lotto 6/49 Number Generator is a free online tool that produces random quick picks for Canada's Lotto 6/49 lottery. The primary keyword is Lotto 6/49 number generator, and the tool follows the official format: 6 numbers from 1–49 and one bonus ball drawn from the same remaining pool.

    Because the bonus ball comes from the leftover numbers after the 6 main draws, it is guaranteed to be different from all your main numbers — just like in the real draw.

    4. How Lotto 6/49 Works

    Lotto 6/49 is Canada's original national lottery, administered by the Interprovincial Lottery Corporation and sold through OLG, BCLC, Loto-Québec, Atlantic Lottery, and Western Canada Lottery. Draws are held every Wednesday and Saturday.

    Six balls are drawn from a pool of 1–49, followed by one bonus ball from the remaining 43 numbers. Matching all 6 main numbers wins the Classic Jackpot (guaranteed at $5 million). Matching 5 main numbers plus the bonus ball wins the second prize. There are 6 prize tiers in total.

    A Gold Ball jackpot is also drawn separately each week, starting at $1 million and growing until won. This generator covers the main 6/49 draw format.
