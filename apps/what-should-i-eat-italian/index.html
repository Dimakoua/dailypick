1. What is the Italian Food Wheel?

Italian Food Wheel is a random dish picker built around the real Italian culinary tradition. It covers Italy's extraordinary range — from the Roman pasta canon and the Neapolitan pizza heritage to the rich ragù of Bologna, the seafood of the Amalfi Coast, and the dolci that finish every Italian meal with something unforgettable.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who loves Italian food and can never decide between pasta, pizza, risotto, or a beautiful piece of grilled fish. Home cooks looking for tonight's recipe inspiration, diners choosing between Italian restaurants, and travellers planning what to eat in Rome, Florence, Naples, or Milan. Spin the wheel, honour the pick.

5. Italian food categories

Italian Classics covers the dishes that define Italian food worldwide — spaghetti carbonara, margherita pizza, risotto alla Milanese, lasagne al forno, tiramisù, arancini, bruschetta, and cannoli. Roman Specialities digs into Rome's extraordinary pasta and meat canon — cacio e pepe, amatriciana, gricia, saltimbocca alla Romana, supplì, trippa alla Romana, coda alla vaccinara, and the beloved maritozzo. Regional Gems travels the country — tagliatelle al ragù from Bologna, mortadella sandwiches from Emilia, parmigiana di melanzane from Naples, spaghetti alle vongole from the coast, bistecca alla Fiorentina from Tuscany, ribollita and panzanella, and the classic torta della nonna. Dolci & Desserts is the sweet finish Italy does best — tiramisù, cannoli, panna cotta, gelato, sfogliatella, zeppole, the perfect affogato, and torrone.
