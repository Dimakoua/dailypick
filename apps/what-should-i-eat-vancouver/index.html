1. What is the Vancouver Food Wheel?

Vancouver Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for the West Coast city. It highlights Vancouver’s seafood, sushi, food trucks, and multicultural neighbourhood dining.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Vancouver locals who want a fast meal choice, visitors exploring the city, and anyone overwhelmed by the endless dining options. Spin and let Vancouver choose the next tasty stop.

3. How it works

Choose a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a pick and announces it. Use the result as a meal prompt, then head to the nearest spot or order in. Spin again if you want a second idea.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Coastal Classics, International Eats, Food Truck Favorites, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real Vancouver picks: Every item is grounded in Vancouver’s real food scene.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Smooth spinning for phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in PNW palettes.

5. Vancouver food categories

Coastal Classics covers dishes that define Vancouver — sushi bentos, spot prawn rolls, seafood chowder, salmon burgers, Granville Island tacos, Japanese curry, tempura bowls, and matcha desserts. International Eats reflects the city’s global kitchens — Japadogs, Gastown brunch, Richmond dim sum, Vietnamese pho, Asian fusion bowls, sushi in Kitsilano, Italian coffee shops, and oyster bars. Food Truck Favorites highlights Vancouver’s market food scene — poutine, loaded fries, gourmet hot dogs, artisan sandwiches, spicy noodle bowls, grilled cheese, and crepe wraps. Neighbourhood Picks points to dining districts — Yaletown steakhouse, Kitsilano café, Granville Island market meals, Commercial Drive pizza, West End vegan, North Shore seafood, Downtown tapas, and Coal Harbour Italian.

6. Tips

Use Coastal Classics for seafood and sushi, International Eats for global flavours, Food Truck Favorites for casual market eats, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a local district. Add your own favourite Vancouver spots for a personalised wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader global food choice, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific pick, use the Indian, Chinese, or Japanese food wheels. Need a drink to pair? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Vancouver’s food scene blends fresh West Coast ingredients with a global restaurant culture. That abundance makes choice hard, and this wheel turns it into one fast, fun decision.
