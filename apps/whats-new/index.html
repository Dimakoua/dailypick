What's New in Daily Pick!

Give your stand-ups instant context. Daily Pick now connects to your project management tools to sync your team and display relevant work, right inside the game.

  • Seasonal Controls: Holiday toggles sit inside their own section with clearer explanations and inline status messages.

Visit Settings & Brand Studio to try the new layout and share player lists or brand presets with your team.

  • Session persistence: Player cursors, deck choice, and story labels stick around even if the host reloads, so you can resume estimation without re-setup.

Open Planning Poker Hub to spin up a new room and try the redesigned flow.
