What’s New at Daily Pick

Daily Pick continues to evolve with smarter integrations, better planning tools, and refreshed workflows for stand-ups, sprint planning, and team pulse checks.

Integrations for faster stand-ups

Connect Daily Pick with your team’s existing workflow so every ritual starts with real context and less setup.

  • Jira, Trello, and GitHub: Import teammates and assigned work directly into the player list, then preview cards, issues, and story context before saving.
  • Slack command support: Launch Daily Pick from Slack with /dailypick, then authenticate in Settings to start Planning Poker, morale checks, or decision sessions instantly.
  • Secure token storage: Connections save safely to the Daily Pick Worker so your access tokens stay protected and reusable across devices.

Enable integrations in Settings → Third-Party Integrations and keep your team rituals connected to the tools you already use.

Planning & estimation tools that work together

New agile tools help teams estimate work, model capacity, and choose priorities without losing momentum.

  • Planning Poker Hub: Run multiplayer estimation sessions with hidden votes, flexible decks, Jira-ready summaries, and a recent estimate history panel.
  • Capacity Planner Dice: Simulate sprint load, account for meetings and time off, and keep planning conversations grounded in real team capacity.

Find these features in the Planning & Estimation section on the home page.

Brand Studio redesign

We reorganized Settings & Brand Studio so customization is easier to manage and faster to preview.

  • Sectioned workflow: Identity, palette, and theme settings are grouped into smaller cards for faster editing.
  • Sticky preview: On larger screens, the live preview stays visible while you make color and emoji updates.
  • Seasonal controls: Holiday theme and style toggles now include clearer descriptions and inline status feedback.

Visit Settings & Brand Studio to explore the new layout and brand preset options.

Planning Poker Hub redesign

Planning Poker Hub now feels cleaner, faster, and more reliable for teams running live estimation sessions.

  • Responsive host controls: Deck selection, round controls, and vote state are now easier to scan across desktop and tablet layouts.
  • Actionable summaries: Revealed results include averages and counts that can be copied into Jira, Slack, or sprint notes.
  • Session persistence: Story labels, deck choices, and player cursors stay available after reloads so you can resume without reconfiguring.

Open Planning Poker Hub to try the refreshed estimation workflow.

Cadence Timer Studio

Build multi-stage meeting timers with sharable presets, audio cues, and color-coded stage labels so every agenda stays on track.

  • Quick timers and cadence presets: Start a single countdown or chain multiple stages with custom labels, durations, and audio prompts.
  • Import and export presets: Save and reuse meeting cadences across teams or share them as files.

Cadence Timer Studio is available to everyone and helps you keep stand-ups, sprint reviews, and check-ins moving smoothly.

Morale Thermometer

Capture anonymous team sentiment with a live visual pulse check, then use the results to make more inclusive, data-driven decisions.

Office Trap refresh

Trap! has been updated with a pixel-inspired office theme, playful avatars, and faster game pacing to keep meetings engaging and fun.

  • New visual style: Retro office desks, carpets, and character sprites create a more immersive experience.
  • Updated pacing: The trap now activates after a short delay so rounds stay quick and exciting.

Speedway Racer refresh

The racer experience now features a wider layout, stronger controls, and a brighter color palette for a clearer, more energetic race.

New seasonal themes

Daily Pick now supports themed experiences for holidays and seasonal rituals. Expect fresh visuals for celebrations and team events throughout the year.

Buttons, headers, accents, and seasonal artwork now update instantly so your brand and rituals feel polished on every screen.

We’re always improving Daily Pick. Share your ideas and feedback at /feedback so we can continue building better tools for your team.
