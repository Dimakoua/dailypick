What's New in Daily Pick!

We've been busy adding new features and making improvements to help your team have more engaging and productive meetings. Here's a look at what's new.

🔌 Integrations with Your Favorite Tools

Give your stand-ups instant context. Daily Pick now connects to your project management tools to sync your team and display relevant work, right inside the game.

  • Jira, Trello & GitHub: Sync your board members or repository contributors directly to the player list, saving setup time. When a player's turn comes up, their assigned tasks or issues automatically appear in the new "Stand-up Dock" for easy reference.
  • Slack slash command: Launch any ritual from Slack with /dailypick, authenticate via the new Settings panel, and let teams jump into Planning Poker or morale checks without leaving chat.

Ready to supercharge your stand-ups? Connect your tools in the new "Integrations" section in your settings.

🎲 New Games for Planning & Estimation

We've added a whole new category of games designed to make your sprint planning and estimation sessions more collaborative and fun.

  • Planning Poker Hub: A real-time estimation tool with hidden votes and Jira-ready summaries.
  • Capacity Planner Dice: Roll dice to stress-test your sprint capacity and account for time off and meetings.

You can find these new games in the "Planning & Estimation" section on the home page.

🎨 Brand Studio Gets Sections

The Settings & Brand Studio page has been reorganized so you can work in smaller chunks instead of scrolling through one massive form.

  • Identity, Palette, and Themes: Each area is grouped into its own mini-card with context about what the fields power.
  • Sticky Preview: On wider screens the live preview now stays visible as you edit, making it easier to see color and emoji changes immediately.
  • Seasonal Controls: Holiday toggles sit inside their own section with clearer explanations and inline status messages.

Visit Settings & Brand Studio to try the new layout and share player lists or brand presets with your team.

🃏 Planning Poker Hub Redesign

The multiplayer Planning Poker experience has been refreshed to feel more modern and easier to host.

  • Flexible layout: Host controls, deck selection, and vote states now live in a streamlined sidebar that adapts to desktop and tablet screens.
  • Clearer summaries: Revealed rounds produce copy-ready summaries with averages and counts that paste directly into Jira or Slack.
  • Session persistence: Player cursors, deck choice, and story labels stick around even if the host reloads, so you can resume estimation without re-setup.

Open Planning Poker Hub to spin up a new room and try the redesigned flow.

⏱️ Cadence Timer Studio

Build multi-stage meeting timers with shareable presets and audio cues to keep your meetings on track. All features are available for everyone to use.

🌡️ Morale Thermometer

Anonymously rate team energy on a visual scale and see the aggregated results in real-time. A great way to gauge the team's mood without putting anyone on the spot.

🪑 Office Trap Gets a Pixel Makeover

The Trap! game has been redesigned as a pixel office full of desks, chairs, and coworkers. Players now appear as fun office avatars, and the trap is a sneaky microphone that turns into a juicy burger to pull people in.

  • Pixelated office theme: New desk layout, widescreen office carpet, and retro sprite rendering.
  • Trap mechanics tuned: Mice are now decorative only, and the trap attracts players after a delay to keep matches fast and tense.

🏁 Speedway Racer UI Refresh

The racer experience has been upgraded with a wider layout, bolder controls, and a more vivid color palette that keeps the action feeling fast.

🎄 New Themes for Holidays

Get into the festive spirit with our new holiday themes! We'll be rolling out special themes for various holidays throughout the year. Keep an eye out for spooky Halloween themes, cheerful Christmas themes, and more.

We hope you enjoy these new features! As always, we'd love to hear your feedback.
