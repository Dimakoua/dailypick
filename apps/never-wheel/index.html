1. What is the Never Wheel?

The Never Wheel is a spin wheel where every possible outcome is a variation of never. It is the only wheel on the internet that offers complete certainty — you will always get never. It honours the procrastinator, the realist, the person who said "I'll start Monday" in 2019, and anyone who has quietly accepted that some things are simply not going to happen.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who needs to confirm what they already know. People who have been meaning to do the thing for three years. Those who need an official ruling from a neutral third party (the wheel) that it is acceptable to not do it. Also great for gifting to a friend who keeps promising to come to your event and definitely won't.

3. How it works

Choose a preset — Classic Never, Strong No, Nuanced Never, or Poetic Never — then spin. The wheel lands on a never. You accept the never. You feel better. The end.
