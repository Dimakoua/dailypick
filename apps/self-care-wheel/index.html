1. What is the Self-Care Wheel?

The Self-Care Wheel is a gentle spinner that gives you a wellness prompt when you need a soft reset. It is designed for small, achievable self-care actions that can fit into the busy parts of your day.

2. Who should use the Self-Care Wheel?

Anyone who wants a quick reminder to slow down, recharge, or treat themselves kindly. It is especially useful during work breaks, low-energy moments, or when you want to add a little calm to your routine.

3. How it works

Select one of the self-care presets, spin the wheel, and follow the resulting prompt. Each outcome is intended to be easy, restorative, and supportive.

4. Features

  • Soft self-care prompts: calming suggestions for mood, movement, rest, and kindness.
  • Curated presets: Choose from Quick Reset, Mindful Moment, Kindness Choice, or Gentle Glow.
  • Responsive canvas wheel: Smooth spinning animation for desktop and mobile.
  • Calming design: pastel colors and relaxed typography create a restful experience.
  • Customizable style: Use Local Brand Studio to personalize the wheel and interface.

5. Self-care wheel presets

Quick Reset is for fast, low-effort actions. Mindful Moment gives you a calming pause. Kindness Choice is all about treating yourself better. Gentle Glow invites small comforts and soothing rituals.

6. Tips

Spin the wheel when you feel stuck, overwhelmed, or just in need of a small lift. Keep it open during work breaks, use it as a mini wellness check-in, or share it with a friend who could use a kind reminder.

7. Alternatives

If you want a cheerful decision wheel instead, try the Positive Wheel. For something more playful, the Wheel Spinner can pick from many custom options. If you need quick productivity help, the Random List Shuffler can choose a simple task for you.
