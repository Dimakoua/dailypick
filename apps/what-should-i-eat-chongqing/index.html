1. What is the Chongqing Food Wheel?

Chongqing Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s intense and flavorful cuisine. It blends hot pot, street food, and local favorites into one fun decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Chongqing locals choosing lunch or dinner, travelers wanting to explore regional food, and anyone craving a bold meal idea. Spin for a fresh Chongqing food suggestion.

5. Chongqing food categories

Spicy Classics covers the city’s signature hot pot, mala noodles, skewers, dan dan noodles, and mapo tofu. Local Comfort includes Chongqing-style fish, braised pork, rice noodles, sticky rice sweets, dumplings, and tangyuan. City Favorites highlights the most popular street foods and local meals. Global-Style Choices brings broader cuisine into the city’s dining landscape.

6. Tips

Choose Spicy Classics for a bold meal, Local Comfort for regional favorites, City Favorites for crowd-pleasing street food, and Global-Style Choices for international variety. Personalize the wheel with your favorite Chongqing restaurants.
