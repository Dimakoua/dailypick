1. What is the NBA Team Wheel?

The NBA Team Wheel is a random NBA team picker that picks one of the league’s 30 teams for you. Choose a preset or use the full league wheel if you want a purely unbiased cup pick.

2. Who should use this wheel?

New fans who want a team, fantasy players deciding a side, friends settling a debate, or anyone who prefers a neutral selection over hometown bias.

3. How it works

Pick a preset, click the spin button, and watch the wheel choose a team. The result appears immediately when the wheel stops.

4. Features

  • Four easy presets: Atlantic Division, Eastern Conference, Western Conference, and All 30 Teams.
  • Mobile friendly: Spin on phone or desktop.
  • Instant winner: The chosen team is displayed as soon as the wheel stops.
  • Custom colors: Use the local brand config to tweak the wheel palette.

5. All 30 NBA Teams

Eastern Conference: Boston Celtics, Brooklyn Nets, New York Knicks, Philadelphia 76ers, Toronto Raptors, Chicago Bulls, Cleveland Cavaliers, Detroit Pistons, Indiana Pacers, Milwaukee Bucks, Atlanta Hawks, Charlotte Hornets, Miami Heat, Orlando Magic, Washington Wizards.

Western Conference: Dallas Mavericks, Denver Nuggets, Golden State Warriors, Houston Rockets, Los Angeles Clippers, Los Angeles Lakers, Memphis Grizzlies, Minnesota Timberwolves, New Orleans Pelicans, Oklahoma City Thunder, Phoenix Suns, Portland Trail Blazers, Sacramento Kings, San Antonio Spurs, Utah Jazz.

6. Tips

Use the Atlantic Division preset when you want a smaller wheel with big-market teams. Pick Eastern or Western Conference when you want one side of the league. Use All 30 Teams for full NBA randomness.

7. Alternatives

Need game day food? Try the What Should I Eat Wheel. Need a quick yes/no on who starts? Use the Yes or No Wheel.

8. Why it works

NBA fandom can be overwhelming with so many teams and storylines. This wheel removes the pressure and gives you one clean, neutral pick.
