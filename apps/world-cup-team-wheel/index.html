1. What is the World Cup Team Wheel?

The World Cup Team Wheel is a free random team picker for FIFA World Cup 2026. It covers all 48 qualified nations spread across six confederations. Spin it to find out which country you're supporting for the tournament, who you're drawing in a sweepstake, or simply which flag you're flying when you can't decide for yourself.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Neutral fans who want to pick a team for the tournament. Watch-party groups who need fair nation assignments for a sweepstake. Friends settling a "best national team" debate. New football fans who want to choose a side without hometown bias. Office pools needing a fair draw. Anyone who just wants the wheel to decide for them.

3. How it works

Choose a preset — Title Contenders for the favourites, a specific confederation for regional loyalty, or All 48 Teams for the full random draw. Hit Spin and the wheel picks your nation. Whatever it lands on, that country is yours for the tournament. No take-backs.

4. Features

  • Six presets: CONMEBOL (South America), UEFA (Europe), CAF (Africa), AFC (Asia), CONCACAF (North/Central America), and Title Contenders.
  • All 48 nations: The full expanded FIFA 2026 field including all three host nations.
  • Mobile-friendly: Spin at the pub, at a watch party, or anywhere the debate starts.
  • Customisable: Load your own list — group-stage rivals, sweepstake names, or your own shortlist.
  • Instant result: Your nation is displayed the moment the wheel stops.
