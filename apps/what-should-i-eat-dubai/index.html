1. What is the Dubai Food Wheel?

Dubai Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for Dubai’s vibrant food scene. It blends Emirati staples, street food, luxury dining, and international cuisine into one playful decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Dubai visitors planning their next meal, locals choosing a dinner destination, and anyone who wants an easy way to explore Dubai’s dining landscape. Spin to get a fresh idea, then head to the closest restaurant or order in.

3. How it works

Select one of the four presets, then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a dining prompt, then enjoy a new Dubai meal idea.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Emirati Favorites, Global Eats, Dubai Experiences, and Local Flavors.
  • Real Dubai picks: Every item reflects the city’s real dining culture.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Dubai-inspired tones.

5. Dubai food categories

Emirati Favorites includes shawarma, mezze, lamb ouzi, machboos, stuffed camel, kunafa, falafel, and seafood buffets. Global Eats reflects Dubai’s international dining scene with biryani, kebabs, sushi, curry, Moroccan tagine, and more. Dubai Experiences covers the city’s destination dining options — marina seafood, desert BBQ, sky-high steakhouses, beach cafés, and luxury brunches. Local Flavors points to iconic neighbourhood food moments, from souks and beaches to Dubai Creek and JBR.

6. Tips

Choose Emirati Favorites for local classics, Global Eats for international variety, Dubai Experiences for destination dining, and Local Flavors to explore city neighborhoods. Swap in your favorite Dubai restaurants to personalize the wheel even more.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific route, try the Indian or Middle Eastern food wheel. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Dubai blends luxury dining with street food and global cuisine, making meal choices overwhelming. This wheel simplifies the decision by turning the city’s best food options into one fun spin.
