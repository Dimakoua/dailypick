World Cup Trivia: The Ultimate Football Quiz

The World Cup Trivia Challenge is a fast-paced quiz designed for football fans who want to test their knowledge of the world's most-watched sporting event. Covering everything from the inaugural 1930 tournament in Uruguay to the modern era of global superstars, it's the perfect way to pass time during half-time or test your expertise.

  • Legendary Players: Questions on Pelé, Maradona, Messi, and more.

1. How to Play the World Cup Quiz

Simply hit 'Start Challenge' to begin. You'll be presented with 10 random multiple-choice questions. Each question has four possible answers, but only one is correct. Your progress is tracked at the top of the card. Once you finish all 10 questions, you'll see your final score and a rank based on your performance.

2. Classic World Cup History

Did you know the first World Cup was held in 1930? Uruguay not only hosted it but won it, beating Argentina in the final. Since then, the tournament has grown into a global phenomenon. Brazil remains the most successful nation with 5 titles, followed closely by Germany and Italy with 4 each.

3. Player Records & Legends

The World Cup is where legends are made. Miroslav Klose holds the record for the most goals in history (16), while Just Fontaine's 13 goals in a single tournament (1958) remains an untouched record. Pelé is still the only player to have won three separate World Cup trophies (1958, 1962, 1970).

4. Iconic Tournament Moments

From the 'Hand of God' in 1986 to the dramatic 2022 final in Qatar, the World Cup has provided some of the most memorable moments in sporting history. Whether it's a underdog story or a dominant performance by a powerhouse nation, every tournament adds a new chapter to the history books of football.

5. Why Play Football Trivia?

Trivia is a great way to improve your memory and learn surprising facts about your favourite sport. Whether you're a casual viewer or a die-hard ultras fan, knowing the history of the game adds depth to every match you watch. Plus, it's a great way to settle debates with friends about who the real football expert is.

6. Challenge Your Friends

Got a perfect score? Take a screenshot and share it with your group chat! The World Cup Trivia Challenge is designed to be mobile-friendly, so you can play it anywhere — at the stadium, in the pub, or on your commute. See if your friends can beat your score or if they need to brush up on their football history.
