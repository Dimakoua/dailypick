Hungry & Undecided?

🍕 What Should I Eat Wheel

Can't pick a restaurant or a dish? Spin the wheel and let it decide your culinary destiny!

How the Food Decision Wheel Works

Deciding what to eat can be surprisingly stressful (it's called decision fatigue!). Our random food picker takes the pressure off by choosing for you. Whether you're looking for dinner ideas, lunch inspiration, or just a fun way to pick a restaurant with friends, the "What should I eat" wheel is here to help.

Popular Uses:

  • The "I Don't Care" Solution: When your partner says "I don't care," let the wheel decide!
  • New Cuisine Discovery: Use our "Global Mix" preset to try something outside your comfort zone.
  • Restaurant Roulette: Enter the names of 5 local spots and spin to see where you're headed.

Customizing Your Meal Wheel

You can easily customize the options. Simply click the Settings to update the list of names (or food items) used by the wheel across all our apps.

Why Use a Random Meal Generator?

Scientific studies suggest that having too many choices can actually make us less happy with our final decision. By narrowing it down to one random result, you eliminate the stress of comparison and turn mealtime back into an enjoyable experience.
