1. What is the Tokio Food Wheel?

Tokio Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s legendary food scene. It combines sushi, ramen, izakaya fare, and modern dining into a fun decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Tokio visitors exploring the city, locals deciding where to eat, and anyone overwhelmed by the city’s many dining choices. Spin for a fresh Tokio food idea.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then enjoy. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Japanese Classics, Tokio Favorites, Global Eats, and City Dining.
  • Real Tokio picks: Every item reflects the city’s food culture and dining scene.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Tokio-inspired tones.

5. Tokio food categories

Japanese Classics includes sushi, ramen, tempura, okonomiyaki, tonkatsu, onigiri, mochi, and yakitori. Tokio Favorites highlights local favorites like sashimi, udon, soba, shabu shabu, and matcha desserts. Global Eats brings international restaurants into the city. City Dining points to upscale restaurants and café dining in Tokio.

6. Tips

Choose Japanese Classics for traditional meals, Tokio Favorites for local picks, Global Eats for international variety, and City Dining for restaurant-style experiences. Add your favorite Tokio restaurants to personalize the wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want cuisine-specific direction, try the Japanese or street-food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Tokio blends historic cuisine with modern dining and global influence. This wheel simplifies the city’s food choices into one fast, fun decision.
