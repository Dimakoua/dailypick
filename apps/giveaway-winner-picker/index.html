Professional Giveaway Winner Picker & Random Draw Tool

Whether you're running a social media contest, a raffle, or a community prize draw, our Giveaway Winner Picker ensures a fair and transparent result. It's designed for streamers, marketers, and community managers who need a reliable way to pick winners from a list of entries.

Why use this Giveaway Randomizer?

  • Fair & Unbiased: Uses a secure randomization algorithm to ensure every entry has an equal chance.
  • Live Drawing Mode: Use "Clear Results" before starting your screen recording or live stream to build anticipation with your audience.
  • Multi-Winner Support: Draw up to 20 winners at once, perfect for grand prizes and runner-up spots.

Best Practices for Social Media Giveaways

To keep your giveaway legal and fair, always provide clear rules and use a transparent tool like Daily Pick for the final draw. Screen recording the drawing process is a great way to prove to your followers that the selection was truly random.

How to export your winners?

Once the winners are selected, use the "Copy Winners" button to instantly save the list to your clipboard. You can then paste it directly into your announcement post, email, or spreadsheet.
