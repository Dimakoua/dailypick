1. What is the Kolkata Food Wheel?

Kolkata Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s legendary food culture. It blends street snacks, Bengali classics, and international tastes into one playful decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Kolkata locals deciding what to eat, visitors exploring the City of Joy, and anyone overwhelmed by the city’s many dining options. Spin for a fresh Kolkata food idea.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Street Classics, Bengali Favorites, City Eats, and Global Choices.
  • Real Kolkata picks: Every item reflects the city’s food culture.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Kolkata-inspired tones.

5. Kolkata food categories

Street Classics covers Kolkata’s iconic snacks — kathi rolls, egg rolls, mishti doi, roshogolla, chingri malai curry, phuchka, telebhaja, and biryani. Bengali Favorites highlights the region’s traditional dishes — macher jhol, shorshe ilish, kosha mangsho, chicken chaap, dal, cholar dal, luchi, and khichuri. City Eats reflects the local restaurant culture — fish fry, bhapa ilish, Tangra Chinese, café food, sweets counters, and tea stall treats. Global Choices brings international dining into Kolkata with dosa, Schezwan noodles, pizza, sushi, kebabs, shawarma, burgers, and BBQ ribs.

6. Tips

Choose Street Classics for Kolkata street food, Bengali Favorites for local comfort dishes, City Eats for neighborhood dining, and Global Choices for international variety. Personalize the wheel with your favourite Kolkata spots as needed.

7. Alternatives

8. Why it works

Kolkata blends delicious street food with rich Bengali cuisine and international dining. This wheel simplifies the choice by turning the city’s best food ideas into one fast, fun spin.
