Brand Studio – Customize your Daily Pick look, color palette, and brand identity

Brand Studio is a brand studio tool that helps teams make Daily Pick feel native to their brand. Use the settings to customize colors, emojis, typography, and seasonal themes across your experience.

This tool lets you update the brand name, brand emoji, font stack, logo URL, and a full palette of accent, surface, and text colors.

Changes apply instantly in the live preview and are stored for every game, so your team sees a consistent visual identity wherever you use Daily Pick.

Brand Studio is built to make stand-ups, retros, and decision tools feel less generic and more like your organization.

1. What Is Brand Studio?

Brand Studio is a brand studio experience for configuring your team’s look and feel inside Daily Pick. It helps you personalize the interface with your own colors, emoji, typography, and logo settings.

Use Brand Studio to make Daily Pick feel like part of your culture, whether you are in a stand-up, planning session, or classroom activity.

The tool supports identity controls for your brand name and mark, a complete palette of surface and accent colors, and seasonal theme toggles for holiday styling.

Brand Studio keeps the customization simple and immediate, so your team can see changes in real time without guesswork.

2. How to Use It?

  1. Update the brand name, emoji, font family, or logo URL in the Brand identity section.
  1. Choose your accent, background, surface, and text colors in the Color palette section.
  1. Toggle seasonal themes on or off, then use Auto or Manual mode to control holiday styling.
  1. Click Save Brand to store your custom theme, or Reset Brand to return to defaults.

3. Key Features & Customization

  • Brand identity: Customize the team name, emoji mark, font family, and logo URL.
  • Color palette: set accent, surface, background, text, heading, subtle text, and border colors.
  • Seasonal themes: enable or disable holiday styling with automatic or manual control.
  • Live preview: see your brand changes in the preview pane before saving.
  • No premium features: all styling controls are available to every user.

4. Seasonal Themes & Live Preview

Brand Studio’s seasonal themes are a standout feature. They let you choose whether holiday looks apply automatically or only when you select a manual theme.

The live preview pane updates immediately as you change colors or emoji, so you can compare palettes and make confident decisions before saving.

Using seasonal themes with preview helps keep your Daily Pick workspace looking polished and relevant for every team moment.

5. Sharing and Exporting

Brand Studio does not yet offer a direct export link, but you can share your configuration by copying the chosen colors and logo URL. Send the information to a teammate or use the same values on another browser to clone the setup.

If you want a full workspace style shared across devices, save your brand settings and use the Brand Studio page as the source of truth for your team.

6. When to Use This Brand Studio?

Use Brand Studio when you want Daily Pick to feel like part of your organization instead of a generic tool. It is ideal for teams who want a polished, consistent visual identity across every session.

  • Customizing colors for your company or classroom theme
  • Adding a team emoji or mark for better recognition
  • Matching Daily Pick to your brand guidelines
  • Turning on seasonal themes for holidays or events
  • Keeping every team member’s interface aligned during meetings

7. We Want to Hear Your Feedback

If you find a bug or want a feature like theme export, please let us know on the Feedback page. Your suggestions help make Brand Studio more useful for every team.

8. Need an Alternative Tool?

If you want to manage player names and integrations instead, try the Team Roster. If you’re looking for a core decision tool instead of styling, the Random Picker Wheel is a great alternative.
