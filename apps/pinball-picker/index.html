AlphaBounce: The Daily Pick Name Picker Experience

Welcome to AlphaBounce, an exciting and visually engaging game from Daily Pick that turns name picking into a pinball showdown. Watch letters tumble down the playfield and see who can match their roster first—it’s perfect for classrooms, agile teams, and anyone who wants a fresh alternative to boring randomizers.

How to Play AlphaBounce

  • Set your list: Drop in your roster—students, teammates, or participants—one per line in the settings panel.
  • Watch the letters fall: The game pulls only the letters it needs for the remaining names, keeping the action fast and fair.
  • Type to match: As letters enter the Target Zone, type the matching name. The buffer highlights your progress so you never lose track.
  • Celebrate the win: Complete a name and it disappears from the board. Clear the list to finish the challenge or keep playing for friendly competition.

Why Teams Love AlphaBounce

  • Interactive name picker: Keeps everyone engaged compared to simple random name wheels.
  • Skill plus chance: Randomized letters add suspense while typing skills keep things competitive.
  • Meeting energizer: Great for deciding who presents next, assigning tasks, or kicking off retros.
  • Classroom friendly: Teachers use it for attendance games, spelling reinforcement, and participation boosts.
