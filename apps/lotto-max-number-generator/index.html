    1. What Is the Lotto Max Number Generator?

    Lotto Max Number Generator is a free online tool that produces random quick picks for the Lotto Max lottery. The primary keyword is Lotto Max number generator, and the tool follows the official format: 7 numbers from 1–50 and one bonus number drawn from the same remaining pool.

    Because the bonus number is drawn from the leftover numbers after the 7 main picks, it is guaranteed to be different from all of your main numbers — accurately replicating the real draw mechanic.

    No sign-up or installation required. Generate, copy, and play in seconds.

    4. How Lotto Max Works

    Lotto Max is Canada's biggest lottery, administered by the Interprovincial Lottery Corporation and sold through provincial lottery corporations including OLG, BCLC, Loto-Québec, Atlantic Lottery, and Western Canada Lottery. Draws are held every Tuesday and Friday.

    Seven balls are drawn from a pool of 1–50, followed by one bonus number from the remaining 43 numbers. Matching all 7 main numbers wins the jackpot, which starts at $10 million and can grow to $70 million before a must-be-won draw is triggered. Matching 6 main numbers plus the bonus wins the second prize. There are 7 prize tiers in total.

    MaxMillions prizes — additional $1-million draws — are added once the main jackpot exceeds $50 million, giving players even more chances to win when the pot is large.
