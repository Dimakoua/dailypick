1. What is the Los Angeles Food Wheel?

Los Angeles Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for LA’s sprawling food scene. It highlights iconic LA meals, beachside street food, celebrity brunch spots, and neighbourhood restaurant moments.

2. Who should use this wheel?

LA locals who want a quick meal idea, visitors seeing the city for the first time, and anyone overwhelmed by endless dining choices. Spin and let LA pick your next tasty stop.

3. How it works

Select a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use the result to choose a meal, then head out or order in. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: LA Classics, International Eats, Food Truck Favorites, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real LA picks: Every item reflects a real LA food scene or district.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in LA-inspired palettes.

5. LA food categories

LA Classics covers the city’s defining meals — taco truck tacos, avocado toast, sushi omakase, poke bowls, street corn, plant-based burgers, Korean BBQ, and In-N-Out. International Eats reflects LA’s diverse cuisine — Santa Monica seafood, West Hollywood brunch, Venice Burritos, Silver Lake ramen, Chinatown dim sum, Koreatown fried chicken, Thai salad, and Italian pasta. Food Truck Favorites highlights market meals — food truck tacos, loaded fries, chicken & waffles, gourmet hot dogs, street pizza, matcha lattes, craft salads, and frozen yogurt. Neighbourhood Picks maps LA by neighbourhood — Hollywood bistros, Beverly Hills steak, Melrose cafés, DTLA sandwiches, Echo Park vegan, Pasadena donuts, Silver Lake coffee, and Marina Del Rey seafood.

6. Tips

Choose LA Classics for iconic city meals, International Eats for global flavours, Food Truck Favorites for casual market dining, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a specific district. Personalize the wheel with your favourite LA spots.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific option, try the Indian, Chinese, or Japanese food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

LA’s food scene is massive and constantly evolving. This wheel turns the city’s abundance into one quick, fun, and delicious decision.
