1. How the Personality Quiz Works

Unlike a trivia quiz, there are no wrong answers here. Each choice you make is weighted toward a specific set of traits: Leadership, Creativity, Clinicality, or Technical Skill. By the end of the seven questions, our algorithm calculates which legendary player most closely mirrors your footballing philosophy.

2. Meet the World Cup Legends

From Pelé's three trophies to Messi's 2022 crowning moment, every legend has a unique story. Franz Beckenbauer redefined the 'Libero' role, while Johan Cruyff brought 'Total Football' to the world stage. Whether you're a goal machine or a midfield general, there's a legend for every type of player.

3. Understanding Different Playstyles

In modern football, roles are more fluid, but the core archetypes remain. The 'number 10' is the creative hub, the 'poacher' lives for the box, and the 'anchor' provides the stability. This quiz helps you understand which of these iconic roles fits your natural instincts.

4. Why Fans Love Personality Quizzes

Football is as much about character as it is about skill. Fans love to see themselves in their idols. Are you as disciplined as Ronaldo? As cool-headed as Zidane? Taking this quiz is a fun way to engage with the history of the game while learning a little more about your own "football DNA."
