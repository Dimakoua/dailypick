1. What is the Istanbul Food Wheel?

Istanbul Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s unique mix of Turkish tradition and global flavors. It blends street food, classic dishes, and international options into one playful decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Istanbul visitors exploring the city, locals choosing a meal, and anyone overwhelmed by the city’s diverse dining scene. Spin to get a fresh Istanbul food idea.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then enjoy. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Classic Turkish, Bosphorus & Street Eats, Local Favorites, and Global Istanbul.
  • Real Istanbul picks: Every item reflects the city’s culinary identity.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Istanbul-inspired tones.

5. Istanbul food categories

Classic Turkish includes simit, döner, pide, lahmacun, baklava, Turkish tea, menemen, and stuffed grape leaves. Bosphorus & Street Eats captures seafood sandwiches, meze, manti, iskender, and tea-house favorites. Local Favorites highlights roast goose, claypot rice, and local snacks. Global Istanbul points to the city’s thriving international dining scene.

6. Tips

Choose Classic Turkish for traditional dishes, Bosphorus & Street Eats for local and coastal picks, Local Favorites for beloved Istanbul meals, and Global Istanbul for international flavor. Add your favorite Istanbul restaurants to personalize the wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want cuisine-specific direction, try the Turkish or street-food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Istanbul blends centuries of food tradition with global culinary influence. This wheel simplifies the city’s choice overload into one fast, fun decision.
