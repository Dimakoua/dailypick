1. What is the Chicago Food Wheel?

Chicago Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built around the real Chicago dining scene. It covers the city's culinary identity — from deep dish and Chicago dogs that define the Windy City to neighborhood classics and street food specials that make Chicago eating so memorable.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Chicagoans who can’t decide between dozens of great options, visitors exploring the city for the first time, and anyone staring down a menu and needing a fast, delicious answer. Spin, choose, go.

3. How it works

Pick one of the four presets, then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use it as a meal prompt — then find the best nearby spot or order in. Spin again if the pick lands too far from where you are.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Chicago Icons, Downtown Dining, Neighborhood Classics, and Street Food.
  • Real Chicago picks: Every item is grounded in Chicago food tradition, neighborhood favorites, or icon status.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops — ideal for deciding on the go.
  • Instant result: Winner is shown clearly after every spin so you can act fast.
  • Customizable colors: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in Chicago flag blue and red.

5. Chicago food categories

Chicago Icons covers the dishes that define the city — deep dish pizza, a Chicago hot dog, Italian beef, Garrett mix popcorn, Maxwell Street Polish, Rainbow Cone, classic chocolate cake, and dolce far niente dessert vibes. Downtown Dining reflects the city’s riverwalk and Loop restaurant scene — rooftop pizza, steakhouses, sushi omakase, riverfront seafood, modern American dining, brewery fare, farm-to-table seasonal menus, and chef tasting experiences. Neighborhood Classics showcases Chicago’s best local neighborhoods — Pilsen tacos, Bronzeville fried chicken, Lincoln Park brunch, Chinatown barbecue pork, Boystown brats, West Loop ramen, Wicker Park bagels, and South Side tamales. Street Food is Chicago at its most casual and iconic — hot dog stands, Italian beef carts, tamales, pizza puffs, soft pretzels, cheese curds, street corn, and falafel wraps.

6. Tips

Use Chicago Icons for a classic local meal, Downtown Dining for a date night or dinner with a view, Neighborhood Classics when you want to eat like a local, and Street Food for fast, satisfying bites. Swap in your own favorite Chicago spots for a fully personalized wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader global food picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a different region or cuisine, check out the Indian Food Wheel, Chinese Food Wheel, or Italian Food Wheel. To pick a drink to go with your meal, What Drink Should I Choose is a great companion.

8. Why it works

Chicago has one of the richest restaurant scenes in the U.S., with iconic classics, immigrant-driven neighborhood flavors, and casual street food all in the same city. That abundance makes choosing hard — this wheel turns the Windy City’s best food moments into a quick, playful decision. One spin, one meal.
