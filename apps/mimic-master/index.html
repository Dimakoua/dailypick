    How it works

    • Set a display name and start a room. Share the invite link with teammates.
    • Enable your camera, then tap “Start reaction round”. A random number or emoji appears after a short delay.
    • Mimic the prompt. As soon as your hands match, your reaction time locks in.
    • The leaderboard updates live so everyone sees the fastest response.
    • Host can start rounds for newcomers only, reset sessions, or clear the leaderboard.

    All gesture detection runs in your browser. Only your display name and reaction results are shared with the room.
