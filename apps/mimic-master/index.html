    How it works

    • Set a display name and start a room. Share the invite link with teammates.
    • Enable your camera, then tap “Start reaction round”. A random number or emoji appears after a short delay.
    • Mimic the prompt. As soon as your hands match, your reaction time locks in.
    • The leaderboard updates live so everyone sees the fastest response.
    • Host can start rounds for newcomers only, reset sessions, or clear the leaderboard.

    All gesture detection runs in your browser. Only your display name and reaction results are shared with the room.

    Fast, Camera-Based Reaction Training for Remote Teams

    Mimic Master turns gesture matching into a live team energizer that is easy to join, easy to host, and built for distributed groups.

    Players tap in with a display name, enable their camera, and mirror visual prompts like numbers, shapes, or emojis using only their hands. The game records reaction times and updates the room leaderboard instantly so everyone stays engaged.

    Perfect for Standups, Retro Warmups, and Icebreakers

    This activity is designed for short, high-energy sessions. It works especially well as a quick remote warmup before planning meetings, retrospectives, or sprint demos when teams need a fast boost of focus and friendly competition.

    Privacy-Friendly, Browser-Based Motion Detection

    All gesture detection runs locally in the user’s browser. The app only shares display names and reaction performance with the room, helping teams keep the experience lightweight and privacy-conscious.
