    1. What Is the Daily Grand Number Generator?

    The Daily Grand Number Generator is a free, browser-based quick pick tool that creates random number combinations matching the official Daily Grand format: 5 main numbers from 1–49 and 1 Grand Number from 1–7. Every pick is statistically equivalent to quick picks generated by official lottery terminal machines.

    I built this because most online generators use Math.random(), which isn't cryptographically secure. This tool uses crypto.getRandomValues() with Fisher-Yates shuffling and rejection sampling to eliminate modulo bias — ensuring every number has an equal chance of being selected.

    Daily Grand is drawn every Monday and Thursday across Canada. The top prize is $1,000 a day for life — one of the most generous lifetime payouts in North American lotteries.

    3. What Are the Daily Grand Rules?

    Daily Grand uses a two-pool format. Players pick 5 main numbers from a pool of 1–49 and 1 Grand Number from a separate, independent pool of 1–7. The Grand Number is drawn from its own pool, which means it can match one of your main numbers — this follows the official game rules.

    • Main pool: 5 unique numbers from 1–49 (white balls)
    • Bonus pool: 1 number from 1–7 (Grand Number, drawn separately)
    • Draw days: Monday and Thursday
    • Top prize: $1,000 a day for life (match 5 + Grand Number)
    • Second prize: $25,000 a year for life (match 5)

    4. How Does the Number Generator Work?

    This generator uses crypto.getRandomValues() — the same cryptographic random number generator browsers use for security-sensitive operations — instead of Math.random(). For the main numbers, it builds a full pool of 1–49, applies a Fisher-Yates shuffle seeded by cryptographic randomness, and takes the first 5 values. For the Grand Number, it draws a separate random number from 1–7. Rejection sampling eliminates modulo bias, and each line is an independent draw.
