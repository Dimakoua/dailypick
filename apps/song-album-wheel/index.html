1. What is the Song & Album Wheel?

The Song & Album Wheel is a music discovery spinner that randomly selects a song or album from a curated list or a custom playlist you provide. It is built to end the "what should I listen to?" loop and make music discovery feel playful and effortless.

2. Who should use the Song & Album Wheel?

Anyone who listens to music. Perfect for commuters choosing their drive soundtrack, DJs setting the vibe before class, roommates agreeing on background music, and anyone looking for a guided, unexpected recommendation.

3. How the Song & Album Wheel works

Pick a genre preset, or type your own playlist. Press the spin button, and the wheel rotates through the list until it lands on one track. The winner is announced on screen in seconds.

4. Features

  • Eight genre presets: Rock, Hip-Hop, Pop, Electronic, Jazz, Country, Metal, and R&B / Soul.
  • Custom playlist input: Paste any comma-separated list of songs or albums.
  • Visual spinning wheel: A colourful canvas-basec wheel with smooth animation.
  • Mobile-ready: Works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

5. Genre Presets

The curated genre presets give you a balanced mix of iconic tracks and modern hits across eight styles:

  • 🎸 Rock Anthems: From Queen to Nirvana — the essential rock canon.
  • 🎤 Hip-Hop Classics: Golden-era and modern greats from Eminem, Kendrick Lamar, Notorious B.I.G., and Nas.
  • ✨ Pop Hits: Chart-toppers from The Weeknd, Ed Sheeran, Billie Eilish, and Harry Styles.
  • 🎧 Electronic: Dancefloor staples from deadmau5, Daft Punk, M83, and Avicii.
  • 🎷 Jazz Standards: Timeless cuts from Miles Davis, John Coltrane, and Chet Baker.
  • 🤠 Country: Dolly Parton, Johnny Cash, Chris Stapleton, and Garth Brooks.
  • 🤘 Metal: Heavy riffs from Metallica, Megadeth, Slayer, and Pantera.
  • 💕 R&B / Soul: Smooth grooves from Daniel Caesar, H.E.R., Miguel, and The Weeknd.

6. Custom Playlist Input

Use the Quick Add box to paste your own list of songs or albums, separated by commas. Hit "Load Playlist" and the wheel immediately updates with your picks. This is great for party setlists, team anthem choices, or guilty-pleasure reveal rounds.

7. Tips

Start with a genre preset if you want a quick spin. Build a custom playlist for themed events or listening parties. Combine genres on the same wheel for variety, and use the result as a conversation starter at gatherings.

8. Alternatives

If you want a broader randomizing experience, try the Choices Spinner for any custom list, or the Random List Shuffler to reorder full playlists. For a different kind of music fun, try Truth or Dare with friends.
