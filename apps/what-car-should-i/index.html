1. What is the Car Wheel?

The What Car Should I wheel is a random car picker for every car situation — buying, renting, dreaming, or just wanting a wildcard suggestion. It covers practical car categories for real-life purchase decisions, dream cars for aspirational scrolling, rental tiers for the next trip, and wildcard options for when you want the wheel to go completely off-script.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who has had 47 browser tabs open comparing cars for three weeks. People standing at the rental desk who can't decide between compact and midsize. Dreamers who want an official wheel ruling on which supercar to fixate on. Anyone who just wants to know what car to drive next without committing to a spreadsheet.

3. How it works

Choose a preset — What to Buy for practical categories, Dream Garage for aspirational picks, Rent This for rental tiers, or Wildcard Drive for a surprise — then hit Spin. The wheel selects your car. Use the result as inspiration, a starting point for research, or just go with it entirely.

4. Features

  • Four presets: What to Buy, Dream Garage, Rent This, and Wildcard Drive — covering every car scenario.
  • Practical to aspirational range: Economy hatchbacks to Ferraris, all on the same wheel.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones — perfect for the rental desk, the dealership forecourt, or the sofa.
  • Instant result: One spin, one car, move to the next step.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in your brand or favourite manufacturer's colours.

5. Wheel categories

What to Buy covers the main car categories for a real purchase decision — SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, Pickup Truck, Minivan, Electric Car, Hybrid, and Sports Coupe. Dream Garage is for aspirational spinning — the Porsche 911, Ferrari, Tesla Model S, Land Rover Defender, Ford Mustang, BMW M3, Lamborghini, and Range Rover. Rent This maps rental tiers from economy to premium — Economy Hatchback, Compact Sedan, Midsize SUV, Full-size SUV, Luxury Sedan, Convertible, Minivan, and Pickup Truck. Wildcard Drive is for the adventurous — Classic Muscle Car, Vintage VW Camper, Supercar for a Day, Off-Road Beast, Electric Hypercar, American Pickup, Italian Sports Car, and Japanese Kei Car.

6. Tips

Use What to Buy when narrowing down a real purchase, Dream Garage when you need to dream big and argue about cars, Rent This at the budget screen of any car hire site to end deliberation instantly, and Wildcard Drive when you want the wheel to take you somewhere unexpected. Add your own shortlist of specific models for a fully personalised car picker.

7. Alternatives

Once the car is sorted, you'll need somewhere to go — try the Vacation Wheel to pick your trip timing. Need to decide what to eat on the road? The What Should I Eat Wheel has the food sorted. For any other big decision, the Yes or No Wheel can settle whether you're actually buying it today.

8. Why it works

Car buying paralysis is one of the most documented forms of decision fatigue — too many makes, models, trims, reviews, and "just one more test drive" loops. This wheel doesn't replace research, but it breaks the deadlock. It gives you a direction to start from instead of an infinite field of options. Pick a category, spin, research that category. One spin, one direction, let's drive.
