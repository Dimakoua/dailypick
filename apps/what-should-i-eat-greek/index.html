1. What is the Greek Food Wheel?

Greek Food Wheel is a random dish picker built around the real Greek culinary tradition. It covers Greece's incredible range — from the mezedes spread and hearty mainland stews to the fresh seafood of the Aegean islands, the smoky grilled meats of a proper Greek barbecue, and the honey-drenched pastries that round off every celebration.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who loves Greek food and can never choose between mezedes, grilled fish, and a rich slow-cooked stew. Home cooks looking for tonight's recipe inspiration, diners at a Greek taverna paralysed by the menu, and travellers planning what to eat in Athens, Santorini, Thessaloniki, or Crete. Spin, choose, Opa!

5. Greek food categories

Greek Classics covers the dishes that define Greek food worldwide — moussaka, souvlaki, spanakopita, tzatziki with warm pita, gyros, a proper horiatiki village salad, baklava, and taramasalata. Seafood & Coastal brings the Aegean to your table — chargrilled octopus, crispy kalamari, fresh sea bream, shrimp saganaki, gavros anchovies, kakavia fish soup, grilled sardines, and the indulgent lobster pasta from the islands. Grills & Mains is the heart of Greek hospitality — slow-baked lamb kleftiko, kokoretsi on the spit, souvla, juicy keftedes meatballs, char-grilled lamb chops, pastitsio, giouvetsi baked pasta, and the rich wine-braised stifado. Sweets & Pastries is the golden end to any Greek meal — baklava dripping with honey and walnuts, crispy loukoumades, creamy galaktoboureko custard pie, rizogalo rice pudding, halva, melomakarona honey cookies, kourabiedes almond shortbreads, and the warm cream-filled bougatsa.
