Raffle Ticket Puller turns your list of names into a fair, visual draw. Enter participants, load the tickets, and pull winners one at a time without bias.

1. What Is Raffle Ticket Puller?

Raffle Ticket Puller is a raffle ticket puller that makes drawings feel fair and engaging. It works by loading a list of names into a drum-style display and selecting a winner at random.

The primary keyword here is raffle ticket puller, and the tool is designed for contests, classroom selections, and team giveaways.

With a simple participant input, automatic ticket counting, and a visible draw history, it keeps the process transparent for every attendee.

This tool is best for low-stakes, repeatable draws where you want to avoid bias and keep the experience fun.

3. Key Features & Customization

  • Custom participant list: type or paste entries as line-separated or comma-separated names.
  • Ticket loader: the Load Tickets button validates and saves the list before drawing.
  • Draw history: the “Drawn so far” section shows names that have already been selected.
  • Reset to defaults: clear the current draw and restore the sample ticket list with one click.
  • No premium features: all raffle functionality is available to every user.

4. Loading Tickets and Tracking Winners

One of the strongest features is the ticket loader. It makes sure the list is ready before you draw, so you can fix formatting issues and avoid empty entries.

The drawn history keeps the process transparent. Every selected name appears in the “Drawn so far” area, making it easy to run multiple rounds without repeating winners.

This combination of loading and tracking helps you run clean, repeatable raffles in classroom, team, or giveaway settings.

5. Sharing and Exporting

Raffle Ticket Puller does not yet provide a direct export link or built-in share feature. To share your setup, copy the ticket list and send it to a teammate, or take a screenshot of the drum and winner result.

If you want others to use the same setup, share the page URL along with your current names list so they can recreate the draw.

6. When to Use This Raffle Ticket Puller?

Use Raffle Ticket Puller whenever you want a fair, visual way to select a winner from a list. It removes bias, keeps the process transparent, and adds a sense of excitement to any draw.

  • Picking a giveaway winner for a contest
  • Choosing a classroom student or helper
  • Randomly selecting a team reward recipient
  • Drawing a volunteer for a task or presentation
  • Running quick, impartial name-based selections
