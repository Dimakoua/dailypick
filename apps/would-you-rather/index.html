Would You Rather: The Icebreaker Choice Game for Teams

Would You Rather turns choice prompts into a quick team icebreaker. Use the built-in options for fast laughs, or write your own prompts to match your group, meeting theme, or classroom activity.

Why this game works

  • Instant engagement: A fun question sparks conversation and gets everyone participating.
  • Customizable prompts: Type your own options or use curated choices for variety.
  • Simple interaction: One click decides the answer when the group can’t agree.

1. What is Would You Rather?

Would You Rather is an icebreaker choice game that presents two options and helps a group decide between them. It’s designed for teams, classrooms, and social groups who want a quick, playful way to start a conversation.

2. Who should use Would You Rather?

Use this game for remote standups, team warm-ups, meeting icebreakers, or casual gatherings. It is ideal for groups that want a lighthearted prompt to break the ice without spending time on setup.

3. How Would You Rather works

Enter two options or use one of the curated prompts, then click choose to reveal a randomly selected answer. Use swap to reverse the choices, or new prompt to bring up a fresh question.

4. Would You Rather features

  • Custom prompts: Type your own options to fit your group or theme.
  • Built-in choices: Generate new prompts instantly with one click.
  • Swap options: Flip the question when you want to see it from the other side.
  • Reset quickly: Start a fresh round anytime without reloading the page.

5. Would You Rather use cases

Would You Rather is great for kickoff meetings, team-building sessions, classroom icebreakers, virtual socials, and quick decision moments when you need a fun prompt.

6. Would You Rather tips

Keep prompts short and clear, choose questions that fit your audience, and use the game to stimulate conversation rather than force a serious answer. Swap options to keep the prompt balanced, and save custom favorites by keeping them in your input fields.

7. We Want to Hear Your Feedback

If you have ideas for better prompts, game modes, or sharing options, please tell us on the Feedback page. Your input helps make Would You Rather better for every team.

8. Need an Alternative Tool?

If you want a decision tool with more than two options, try Choices Spinner. For simple yes/no choices, Yes or No Wheel is another useful option.
