Meal Decision

🍳 Cook or Order In

Can’t decide whether to prepare dinner or order something tasty? Spin the wheel for a fair call.

Choose Cook vs. Order with Confidence

When the dinner question feels overwhelming, a simple random decision can remove the second-guessing. Use this wheel to get a fast, unbiased choice and move on to planning the meal.

How to Use It

  • Feeling tired: Let the wheel choose if tonight should be takeout or a quick home-cooked meal.
  • Short on time: Spin once and commit to the result, then enjoy the stress-free decision.
  • Group dinners: Use it with friends or family when everyone says “I don’t care.”

Why this works

Decision fatigue comes from weighing too many options. By boiling the choice down to two equally possible outcomes, this tool turns uncertainty into action.
