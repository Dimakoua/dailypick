The Ultimate Truth or Dare Prompt Generator

Whether you're hosting a sleepover, a birthday party, or just hanging out with friends, Truth or Dare is the go-to game for entertainment. Our online generator provides a seamless way to play without having to think of questions on the spot.

Why play Truth or Dare online?

  • Zero Prep: No need to write down prompts.
  • Variety: Choose from different modes like Classic, Party, or Deep to match the vibe.
  • Fairness: The randomizer ensures everyone gets a mix of easy and challenging prompts.

Game Rules

The rules are simple: one player chooses "Truth" or "Dare." If they choose Truth, they must answer a question honestly. If they choose Dare, they must perform a task. If they refuse, there's usually a "penalty" decided by the group (like doing 10 pushups!).

Perfect for Remote Teams

Our "Classic" mode is also great for team building icebreakers. It helps colleagues get to know each other in a lighthearted, professional way during virtual hangouts.
