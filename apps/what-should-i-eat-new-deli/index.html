1. What is the New Deli Food Wheel?

New Deli Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for the capital’s legendary food scene. It highlights Delhi’s classic curries, market snacks, and neighbourhood favorites.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Delhi locals who want a fast meal idea, visitors exploring the city, and anyone overwhelmed by the many delicious choices. Spin and let New Deli pick your next bite.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use the result as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again for another delicious option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Delhi Classics, Street Favorites, International Delhi, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real Delhi picks: Every item reflects the city’s authentic dining culture.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in New Deli colors.

5. New Deli food categories

Delhi Classics covers the city’s iconic fares — butter chicken, chole bhature, paneer tikka, chicken tikka masala, biryani, paratha, rajma chawal, and chaat. Street Favorites highlights popular market snacks — kebabs, naan, parathas, rolls, street chaat, mithai, samosas, and kulfi. International Delhi reflects the city’s global food scene — Punjabi lassi, dosa, idli, vada pav, momos, Chinese Hakka noodles, shawarma, and shahi paneer. Neighbourhood Picks points to Delhi districts — Karim’s kebabs, Old Delhi brunch, Connaught Place cafés, South Delhi thalis, Karol Bagh sweets, Chandni Chowk snacks, Gurgaon grills, and Langar at Gurudwara temples.

6. Tips

Choose Delhi Classics for hearty comfort food, Street Favorites for market snacks, International Delhi for global eats, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a district. Personalize the picks with your favorite New Deli spots.

7. Alternatives

For a broader food picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific choice, try the Indian, Chinese, or Japanese food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

New Deli’s food scene is bold, rich, and full of variety. This wheel turns that abundance into one fast, fun, delicious decision.
