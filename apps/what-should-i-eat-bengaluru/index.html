1. What is the Bengaluru Food Wheel?

Bengaluru Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s fast-changing dining scene. It blends South Indian favorites, local classics, and global cuisine into a simple, fun decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Bengaluru locals choosing lunch, tech workers planning dinner, and visitors exploring the city’s food culture. Spin to get a fresh Bengaluru meal idea without scrolling through endless menus.

3. How it works

Select a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again for another choice.

4. Features

5. Bengaluru food categories

South Indian Classics covers dosa, idli, uttapam, rava dosa, Mysore bonda, filter coffee, medu vada, and kesari bhath. Global Bengaluru includes kebabs, biryani, shawarma, Chinese Manchurian, Thai curry, sushi, pasta, and burgers. Café & Nightlife reflects the city’s coffee culture and night eating scene. Local Favorites highlights Bengaluru-specific dishes like Konark biryani, benne dosa, bisi bele bath, and street rolls.

6. Tips

Choose South Indian Classics for comfort food, Global Bengaluru for international variety, Café & Nightlife for café favorites and bar snacks, and Local Favorites for iconic Bangalore dishes. Add your favorite local restaurants to personalize the wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific route, try the Indian or South Indian food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Bengaluru blends traditional South Indian cuisine with a modern global dining scene. This wheel simplifies the choice by turning the city’s best meal ideas into one fast, fun spin.
