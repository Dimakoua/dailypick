    1. What Is the Lucky for Life Number Generator?

    Lucky for Life Number Generator is a free online tool that produces random quick picks for the Lucky for Life lottery. The primary keyword is Lucky for Life number generator, and the tool is built around the official rules: 5 numbers from 1–48 and one Lucky Ball from 1–18.

    The Lucky Ball pool is smaller than those of Powerball or Mega Millions, which means the odds for certain prize tiers are more favourable — making the game a popular choice for players who prioritise consistent wins over record-breaking jackpots.

    No installation or sign-up needed. Just generate, copy, and play.

    4. How Lucky for Life Works

    Lucky for Life is a multi-state US lottery game drawn every Monday and Thursday. Players choose 5 numbers from 1–48 and a Lucky Ball from 1–18. Matching all 5 main numbers plus the Lucky Ball wins the top prize of $1,000 a day for life. The second prize — 5 main numbers without the Lucky Ball — pays $25,000 a year for life.

    There are 10 prize tiers in total. Unlike lump-sum jackpots, the top two prizes are paid as lifetime annuities, making Lucky for Life stand out among major US lotteries.

    Tickets are available across 26 states and Washington D.C. This generator replicates the exact pool sizes so your quick pick works at any participating retailer.
