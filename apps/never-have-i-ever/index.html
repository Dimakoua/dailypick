1. What Is Never Have I Ever?

Never Have I Ever is a prompt generator for the classic icebreaker game where participants share whether they have done a given activity. The primary keyword is icebreaker prompt generator, and the tool helps groups start conversations with minimal setup.

The game displays a new statement each time you tap “New prompt,” and players respond by revealing whether they have experienced it.

You can also add custom prompts to make the experience more personal, relevant, or work-appropriate depending on your group.

2. How to Use It?

  1. Open Never Have I Ever and press “New prompt” to reveal a statement.
  2. Ask the group to respond honestly based on their own experiences.
  1. Add your own customized prompt using the text input if you want a more tailored game.
  1. Use the reset button to clear the prompt list when you want to start a fresh round or remove custom prompts.

3. Key Features & Customization

  • Random prompts: get fresh statements instantly for fast gameplay.
  • Custom prompt support: add your own experiences to personalize the game.
  • Group-friendly: works well for remote teams, classrooms, and social events.
  • Easy reset: clear custom prompts and start a new round quickly.
  • Accessible design: simple controls and clear prompt display for any group.

4. Gameplay and Prompt Style

Never Have I Ever focuses on fun, relatable prompts that encourage sharing without making anyone uncomfortable. The prompt style is easy to understand and keeps the game moving, which is key for group icebreakers and meeting energy.

By mixing default statements with your own customized prompts, you can tailor the experience for your team, training session, or casual gathering.

The game works best when you choose inclusive prompts and set a friendly tone for the group.

5. Sharing and Exporting

Never Have I Ever does not export prompts directly, but it is easy to share the page link with teammates and play together remotely. You can also copy or note custom prompts if you want to reuse them later.

The prompt generator is lightweight and browser-based, making it simple to launch in meetings or social sessions without extra tools.

6. When to Use This Icebreaker?

Use Never Have I Ever when you want a quick, low-pressure way to build connection through shared stories. It helps break the ice, get people talking, and add a playful moment to your gathering.

  • During team meetings to kick off a conversation.
  • In remote workshops to get participants engaged.
  • At social events or virtual hangouts for lighthearted fun.
  • With classrooms to build rapport and collaboration.
  • As a warmup before retros, planning sessions, or team-building activities.
