Sprint Retrospectives

Collaborative Retro Board

Add cards to reflect on what went well, what to improve, and action items. Vote on insights, drag to organize, and collaborate in real-time.

Why use a Retro Board?

Retrospectives are a cornerstone of agile development. The Retro Board makes it easy to capture team insights, prioritize improvements, and create actionable plans for the next sprint.

  • Structured reflection: Three default columns guide your retro flow.
  • Voting system: Let the team prioritize what matters most.
  • Markdown support: Add code snippets and formatted text.
  • Real-time collaboration: See updates as teammates add cards.
  • Drag & drop: Organize cards and move between columns seamlessly.
