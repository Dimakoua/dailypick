Why Planning Poker?

Story-pointing sessions work best when everyone plays at the same time. The Planning Poker Hub keeps teammates focused with a clean interface, optional story label, and clear call-to-actions for the facilitator.

  • No downloads: Share a link and you’re ready to vote.
  • Hidden until reveal: Cards stay secret until the host reveals them.
  • Smart summaries: Copy averages, vote counts, and names straight into Jira.
  • Team-friendly: If the host drops, the room automatically promotes the next participant.
