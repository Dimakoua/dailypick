    1. What Is the Mega Millions Number Generator?

    Mega Millions Number Generator is a free online tool that produces random quick picks for the Mega Millions lottery. The primary keyword is Mega Millions number generator, and the tool follows official game rules: 5 numbers from 1–70 and one Mega Ball from 1–25.

    It uses Fisher-Yates shuffling driven by crypto.getRandomValues(), giving you a statistically fair, non-repetitive pick every time.

    No account or download is needed. Generate your numbers in seconds and head straight to the counter.

    4. How Mega Millions Works

    Mega Millions drawings are held every Tuesday and Friday. Players select 5 white balls from 1–70 and 1 gold Mega Ball from 1–25. Matching all 5 white balls plus the Mega Ball wins the jackpot. There are 9 prize tiers — even matching just the Mega Ball alone wins $2.

    The optional Megaplier (2×–5×) multiplies non-jackpot prizes. Tickets are available in 45 states plus Washington D.C. and the US Virgin Islands.

    This generator replicates the exact pool sizes — 70 white balls and 25 gold Mega Balls — so your quick pick is formatted correctly for any ticket purchase.
