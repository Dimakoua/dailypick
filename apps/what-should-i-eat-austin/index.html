1. What is the Austin Food Wheel?

Austin Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for Austin’s vibrant food scene. It covers the city’s most iconic eats — from breakfast tacos and barbecue to food trucks and local neighbourhood favourites.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Austinites who need a fast meal decision, visitors exploring the city, and anyone overwhelmed by the endless options. Spin and let Austin choose your next tasty stop.

3. How it works

Select a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use it as a meal prompt and head out or order in. Spin again if the pick lands somewhere too far.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Austin Classics, BBQ & Smokehouse, Food Truck Favorites, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real Austin picks: Every item reflects an authentic Austin dining moment.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in Austin color palettes.

5. Austin food categories

Austin Classics covers the dishes locals love — breakfast tacos, brisket sandwiches, chicken fried steak, pie and mash, kolaches, chili cheese fries, Tex-Mex enchiladas, and pecan pie. BBQ & Smokehouse brings Austin’s legendary smokehouse scene to the wheel — BBQ brisket, smoked ribs, pulled pork, brisket tacos, smoked sausage, BBQ nachos, chicken quarters, and mac and cheese. Food Truck Favorites highlights vibrant market eats — truck tacos, loaded fries, chowder, slider trios, Asian fusion bowls, plant-based burgers, grilled cheese, and craft chips. Neighbourhood Picks points you to Austin dining districts — East Austin pizza, SoCo burgers, urban coffee brunch, South Lamar sushi, Deep Eddy Mexican, Dripping Springs farm meals, Allandale pho, and Downtown noodles.

6. Tips

Choose Austin Classics for local comfort food, BBQ & Smokehouse for smoked meats, Food Truck Favorites for casual market dining, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a part of the city. Personalise further with your favourite Austin spots.

7. Alternatives

For a broader food choice, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific pick, try the Indian, Chinese, or Mexican food wheels. Need a drink to go with it? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Austin is famous for its live music and bold food culture, which makes choosing a meal harder, not easier. This wheel turns that richness into a single fast decision so you can spend less time deciding and more time eating.
