1. What is the Chennai Food Wheel?

Chennai Food Wheel is a random meal picker built for the city’s coastal and South Indian dining culture. It blends traditional meals, local specialties, and global cuisine into an easy, fun decision tool.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Chennai locals deciding where to eat, visitors exploring the city, and anyone overwhelmed by endless dining choices. Spin for a fresh Chennai food idea.

3. How it works

Select a preset then press Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and reveals it clearly. Use the result as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again for another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: South Indian Classics, Coastal & Spicy, Street Favorites, and Global Choices.
  • Real Chennai picks: Every item reflects the city’s food culture.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Chennai-inspired tones.

5. Chennai food categories

South Indian Classics covers dosa, idli, uttapam, filter coffee, and sweets like payasam. Coastal & Spicy reflects Chennai’s seafood heritage with crab curry, meen kozhambu, chicken 65, and parotta. Street Favorites highlights easy eats like chaat, vada, sandwich, and rolls. Global Choices brings international dining into the city with pasta, sushi, shawarma, burgers, and Thai curry.

6. Tips

Choose South Indian Classics for comfort food, Coastal & Spicy for regional Chennai flavors, Street Favorites for quick eats, and Global Choices for international variety. Add your favorite local spots to personalize the wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader meal picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want cuisine-specific direction, try the South Indian or street-food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Chennai blends coastal cuisine, South Indian tradition, and modern dining. This wheel simplifies the choice by turning the city’s best meal ideas into one fast, fun spin.
