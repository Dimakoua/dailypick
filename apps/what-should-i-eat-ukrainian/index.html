1. What is the Ukrainian Food Wheel?

Ukrainian Food Wheel is a random dish picker built specifically for Ukrainian and Eastern European cuisine. It covers the full spectrum — from slow-simmered beet soups and hand-folded dumplings to open-fire grills, honey meads, and fermented drinks.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who loves Ukrainian food and faces the daily paralysis of choosing between too many delicious options. Whether you are cooking at home, visiting a Ukrainian restaurant, or hosting a festive dinner, the wheel cuts through the indecision instantly.

3. How it works

Choose one of the four category presets, then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a dish or drink and announces it with a Смачного! — Ukrainian for "bon appétit". Spin again if you want another suggestion, or head straight to the kitchen.

4. Features

  • Four category presets: Ukrainian Classics, Mains & Grills, Soups & Starters, and Drinks & Sweets.
  • Authentic dish names: Items are real, well-known Ukrainian dishes used in everyday and festive cooking.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Смачного result display: Winner is shown with Ukrainian warmth after every spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in Ukraine's sunflower yellow and sky blue.

5. Ukrainian food categories

Ukrainian Classics covers iconic staples — Borscht, Varenyky, Holubtsi, Chicken Kyiv, Deruny, Pampushky, and the beloved salo. Mains & Grills includes Shashlik, Kovbasa, Mlyntsi, and the rich Hutsul polenta dish Banosh. Soups & Starters features Solyanka, Okroshka, Kapusnyak, and the green summer Zelenyy Borshch. Drinks & Sweets rounds out the experience with Uzvar, Kvass, Kompot, Medovukha, and the warming spiced Varenukha.

6. Tips

Use Ukrainian Classics for a comforting weeknight dinner, Soups & Starters when the weather turns cold, Mains & Grills for weekend gatherings, and Drinks & Sweets to finish a festive meal. Add your favourite local Ukrainian restaurant's dishes to spin between real nearby options.
