Why run a morale pulse?

Planning and retros are more productive when everyone feels heard. The Morale Thermometer gives every teammate a safe tap-in so you can surface blockers, burnout, or wins before they surprise you mid-sprint.

  • Anonymous by default: Nobody sees who voted which number.
  • Shareable sessions: Send the link in Slack or your calendar invite.
  • Actionable context: Use the average to justify scope changes or follow-up chats.
