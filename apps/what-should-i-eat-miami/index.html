1. What is the Miami Food Wheel?

Miami Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for the city’s tropical food scene. It features iconic Miami eats, Latin-inspired dishes, beachside flavors, and neighbourhood restaurant picks.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Miami locals who want a fast meal choice, visitors exploring the city, and anyone overwhelmed by Miami’s many dining options. Spin and let Miami choose the next tasty stop.

3. How it works

Pick a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use it as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again if you want a second Miami idea.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Miami Classics, International Eats, Food Truck Favorites, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real Miami picks: Every item reflects the city’s vibrant food culture.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel in Miami palettes.

5. Miami food categories

Miami Classics covers the city’s signature dishes — Cuban sandwiches, stone crab, empanadas, key lime pie, ceviche, plantain chips, chicken and waffles, and arepas. International Eats reflects Miami’s Latin and global dining scene — Caribbean jerk, Peruvian lomo saltado, Brazilian feijoada, Mexican tacos, Argentinian steak, Cuban ropa vieja, Haitian griot, and Colombian bandeja. Food Truck Favorites highlights Miami’s market eats — food truck tacos, street corn, loaded fries, seafood rolls, shawarma, dragon fruit smoothies, poke bowls, and gelato. Neighbourhood Picks points to city districts — South Beach brunch, Brickell sushi, Little Havana café, Wynwood pizza, Coral Gables seafood, Design District salad, Midtown burger, and Key Biscayne café.

6. Tips

Choose Miami Classics for the city’s signature flavors, International Eats for Latin and global dishes, Food Truck Favorites for casual market bites, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a part of the city. Personalize with your favorite Miami spots for a custom wheel.

7. Alternatives

For a broader food picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want a cuisine-specific option, try the Indian, Chinese, or Japanese food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Miami’s food scene blends beachside freshness with Latin energy. This wheel turns that abundance into one fast, delicious decision.
