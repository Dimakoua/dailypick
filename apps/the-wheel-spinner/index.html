Decision Making

Choices Spinner

Add options, spin the wheel, and let randomness decide. Perfect for lunch, topics, priorities, or any team choice.

Pick images from your computer. Each becomes a spinner option using its filename as the label.

Use Label|image-url to attach a remote icon, or upload your own photo options from your computer.

Struggling to pick a fair winner for your giveaway in front of a live audience?

This interactive choices spinner lets you add your options and let randomness decide. Paste your list, spin the wheel, and keep your audience engaged while you get a fair result instantly.

How to Use It

  1. Enter your options in the left input box. Use one item per line or separate entries with commas.
  1. Click 🔄 Update to redraw the wheel and save your current list.
  1. Press 🎡 SPIN! to launch the spinner and let the wheel choose one option.
  1. Read the selected item in the result box below the wheel.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my own options?

Just type them into the text box. You can put one option on each line or separate them using commas. Then, click the Update button to generate your new wheel.

Can I upload images to the wheel?

Yes, use the photo upload button below the text area. You can pick images from your computer, and each image becomes a slice on the wheel using its filename as a label.

Is there a limit to how many options I can add?

There is no hard limit, though adding too many items (like over 100) might make the slices very thin and hard to read. It works best with a manageable list of choices.
