    1. What Is the EuroMillions Number Generator?

    EuroMillions Number Generator is a free online tool that produces random quick picks for the EuroMillions lottery. The primary keyword is EuroMillions number generator, and the tool is built around the official game rules: 5 numbers from 1–50 and 2 Lucky Stars from 1–12.

    It uses Fisher-Yates shuffling on a full number pool seeded by crypto.getRandomValues(), giving you a statistically fair pick every single time. The 2 Lucky Stars are drawn from their own independent pool and are guaranteed to be unique.

    No sign-up is required. Generate your numbers, copy them, and play — it takes under five seconds.

    4. How EuroMillions Works

    EuroMillions is drawn every Tuesday and Friday across 17 participating European countries including the UK, France, Spain, Germany, Italy, and others. Players pick 5 main numbers from 1–50 and 2 Lucky Stars from a separate pool of 1–12. A jackpot winner must match all 5 main numbers plus both Lucky Stars.

    The jackpot starts at €17 million and can grow to a capped maximum of €250 million. There are 13 prize tiers in total — matching just 1 main number and 2 Lucky Stars wins the lowest prize. Tickets are sold through national lottery operators in each participating country.

    This generator replicates the exact pool sizes so your quick pick is formatted correctly for any retailer or official app entry.
