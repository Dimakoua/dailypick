1. What is the Berlin Food Wheel?

Berlin Food Wheel is a random restaurant and dish picker built for the city’s bold food scene. It brings together Berlin classics, international street food, market meals, and neighborhood favorites.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Berliners who want a fast meal decision, visitors exploring the capital, and anyone overwhelmed by the variety. Spin and let Berlin pick your next bite.

3. How it works

Choose a preset then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a suggestion and announces it. Use it as a meal prompt, then head out or order in. Spin again if you want another option.

4. Features

  • Four city presets: Berlin Classics, International Eats, Food Truck Favorites, and Neighbourhood Picks.
  • Real Berlin picks: Every item is based on real city dishes and neighborhoods.
  • Mobile-friendly canvas wheel: Spins smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Instant result: Winner appears clearly after each spin.
  • Customisable colours: Use Local Brand Studio to theme the wheel with Berlin style.

5. Berlin food categories

Berlin Classics covers the city’s iconic eats — currywurst, döner kebab, schnitzel, pretzels, bratwurst, Käsekuchen, spätzle, and apfelstrudel. International Eats reflects Berlin’s diverse cuisine — Vietnamese pho, Turkish lahmacun, Italian pizza, sushi bowls, Greek salad, Middle Eastern mezze, Indian curry, and Spanish tapas. Food Truck Favorites highlights market meals — street food fries, loaded currywurst, döner wraps, ramen snacks, bulgogi tacos, Polish pierogi, and cold brew coffee. Neighbourhood Picks points to Berlin districts — Kreuzberg cafés, Prenzlauer Berg brunch, Friedrichshain burgers, Mitte bistros, Charlottenburg steak, Neukölln pizza, and Wedding döner.

6. Tips

Choose Berlin Classics for local comfort food, International Eats for global flavors, Food Truck Favorites for casual market dining, and Neighbourhood Picks when you want to explore a district. Personalize the wheel with your favorite Berlin spots.

7. Alternatives

For a broader food picker, try What Should I Eat Wheel. If you want cuisine-specific options, use the Indian, Chinese, or Japanese food wheels. Need a drink pairing? Try What Drink Should I Choose.

8. Why it works

Berlin’s food scene is bold, international, and constantly changing. This wheel turns that variety into one fast, fun decision.
