1. What is the Colombian Food Wheel?

Colombian Food Wheel is a random dish picker built for Colombian and Andean cuisine. It covers the full range — from the iconic bandeja paisa piled high with beans, rice, chicharrón, and arepa, to the warming herb-rich ajiaco soup, open-fire lomo al trapo, coastal arroz con coco, and sweet caramel-thick arequipe.

2. Who should use this wheel?

Anyone who loves Colombian food and can't pick from the rich, diverse spread of options. Whether you are cooking at home, visiting a Colombian restaurant, ordering from a food truck, or just craving something hearty, the wheel cuts through the indecision instantly.

3. How it works

Choose one of the four category presets, then hit Spin. The wheel lands on a dish or drink and announces it with a ¡Buen provecho! — Colombian for "bon appétit". Spin again for another suggestion, or head straight to the kitchen.

4. Features

  • ¡Buen provecho! result display: Winner is shown with Colombian warmth after every spin.

5. Colombian food categories

Colombian Classics covers the iconic staples — Bandeja Paisa, warming Ajiaco, Arepa, golden Empanada, hearty Sancocho, slow-roasted Lechona, and the milky Changua breakfast soup. Mains & Grills includes flame-wrapped Lomo al Trapo, smoky Fritanga platters, crispy Chicharrón, Sobrebarriga, and blood-rich Morcilla. Soups & Sides features Caldo de Costilla, Tripe Mondongo, twice-fried Patacones, cheesy Papas Chorreadas, coconut Arroz con Coco, and sweet Plátano Maduro. Drinks & Sweets rounds out the experience with anise-sharp Aguardiente, world-famous Café Colombiano, fruity Lulada, fermented Chicha, thick Arequipe, and the crispy wafer Obleas.

6. Tips

Use Colombian Classics for a full comforting meal, Soups & Sides when you want something lighter or as an accompaniment, Mains & Grills for a weekend asado, and Drinks & Sweets to finish any occasion. Add your favourite local Colombian restaurant's specials for the most practical daily spin.

8. Why it works

Colombian cuisine spans four distinct geographic regions — Andean highlands, Pacific coast, Caribbean coast, and Amazon lowlands — making the choice overwhelming in the best possible way. This wheel turns that abundance into a fast, joyful ritual. One spin, one dish, ¡Buen provecho!
