1. How to pick a World Cup team to support

There are only a few legitimate ways to choose a World Cup team when your own country isn't in it: pick the best team, back the most romantic underdog, support a nation with a connection to you, or let chance decide entirely. This wheel covers all of those options. Choose your preset and spin — your team for FIFA World Cup 2026 will be waiting when the wheel stops.

2. Backing the favourites

The Favourites & Contenders preset includes Argentina (defending champions), France, Spain, England, Brazil, Germany, Portugal, Netherlands, Morocco, Japan, Colombia, and Uruguay — teams that realistically could lift the trophy. If you want to follow a genuine title race, this is your preset. The wheel picks one; you follow them all the way to the final.

3. Supporting an underdog

The Underdogs & Dark Horses preset is where the real World Cup magic lives. Morocco's run to the semi-finals in 2022. Japan beating Germany and Spain in the group stage. Senegal winning AFCON. These are teams capable of shocking the world, and backing one of them from the group stage makes every win feel enormous. Spin this preset for a tournament you'll actually remember.

4. Cheering for a host nation

FIFA World Cup 2026 has three host nations: the United States, Canada, and Mexico. All three qualify automatically and all three have passionate, growing football cultures. The USA is a genuine dark horse with a talented young squad. Canada is on an upward trajectory. Mexico brings decades of World Cup history and some of the loudest fans on the planet. Spin the Host Nations preset to back the home crowd.

5. Picking by confederation

If you have a regional soft spot — a love of South American football, or a preference for European club pedigree — use the South America or Europe presets. These give you a team within a region you already follow, making the World Cup feel like an extension of the football you watch all season.

6. Running a sweepstake

The Any Nation preset includes all 48 qualified teams. Gather your group, spin once per person, and assign each result as their sweepstake nation. Whoever's team lifts the trophy wins the pot. Spin until every person has a unique country — just close the tab and reopen if you want to reset.

7. Alternatives

Want to see all 48 teams? Use the World Cup Team Wheel. Picking a host city to visit? Try the World Cup Host City Wheel. Need match-day food? The What Should I Eat Wheel has you covered. Can't decide whether to watch alone or in a group? The Yes or No Wheel settles it.

8. Why random team assignment works

When you choose a team yourself, you always second-guess it. Did I pick them because they'll win? Because their kit is nice? Because they knocked out someone I don't like? Random assignment removes all of that. The wheel picked your team. Now your job is to learn their players, find out when they play, and invest. You'd be surprised how quickly you start caring about a team the wheel assigned you.
