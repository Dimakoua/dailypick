## The Privacy Problem No One Talks About

Before you even spin a wheel on Wheel Decide, a consent banner appears disclosing that **1,716 partners** may store data from your device for advertising and tracking purposes. That's not a minor footnote—that's an aggressive data brokerage operation wrapped around a simple spinner.

If you're using Wheel Decide in a classroom, in a company meeting, or on a shared screen, every person in the room is subject to that data collection.

Daily Pick collects **nothing**. No cookies, no tracking, no consent banners. You open a tool and use it. That's the whole experience.

## The Login Wall

Wheel Decide requires an account to save any custom wheel you build. Forget to log in first and your work is gone on refresh. For spontaneous team decisions—"quick, let's pick who presents first"—that friction is a real problem.

Daily Pick requires **no account for anything**. Every tool is instant-access. Session-based multiplayer apps like [Planning Poker](/apps/planning-poker/) and [Retro Board](/apps/retro-board/) run in real time without any sign-up, ever.
