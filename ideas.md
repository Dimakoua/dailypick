
    Current Project Snapshot

    Daily Pick is a collection of ~112 apps across 8 categories: Stand-up Games (7), Agile Tools (5), Randomizers (16), Party Games
    (21), Food Wheels (35), Lottery (9), FIFA 2026 (8), and Nav/Utility (11). It runs on Eleventy + Cloudflare Workers with Durable
    Objects for multiplayer features.
