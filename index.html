Streamline Your Stand-ups!

Make daily stand-ups and team decisions more engaging and fair with Daily Pick! Choose from our collection of fun interactive tools like the Decision Wheel, Speedway Racer, or the strategic Trap! Game to set speaking order, pick names, or just enjoy a quick team-building moment. Say goodbye to "who's next?" and hello to fun!

🎡

Wheel

A fair and fun spin to decide who shares updates next. Quick, easy, and no more 'who's first?' dilemmas! 🐢

Speedway

A delightful turtle race to determine your team's stand-up sequence. May the best colleague (and their turtle) win! 🎯

Trap!

A lighthearted game of strategy. Great for a quick team brain-teaser or deciding who tackles the next coffee run! 🕹️

Falling Letters Pinball

Match names by typing letters as they tumble down a pinball playfield. A fun test of speed and accuracy! 🚀

Gravity Drift

Launch rockets and navigate asteroid fields. A space-themed adventure of trajectory and fuel management! 🔴

Ball Game

A real-time collaborative game where everyone pushes a ball around a shared canvas. Teamwork makes the dream work!

Why Daily Pick?

At Daily Pick, we believe that even routine tasks like daily stand-ups or making small team choices can be opportunities for engagement and fun. Our suite of simple, browser-based tools is designed to inject a bit of playfulness into your workday, fostering a more positive and collaborative environment.

Whether you're part of an Agile/Scrum team looking for a fair way to determine speaking order, a teacher needing a random student selector, or just want a quick, unbiased decision-maker, Daily Pick has a tool for you. Our games are:

  • Easy to Use: No complicated setups, just open and play.
  • Fair & Unbiased: Leveraging randomness to ensure equitable selections.
  • Engaging: Turning potentially mundane moments into interactive experiences.
  • Versatile: Useful for various scenarios beyond just daily meetings.

Explore our tools and see how a little bit of fun can make a big difference in your team's dynamics and productivity. We're always thinking of new ways to help teams connect and make decisions smoothly!
